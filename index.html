Vision

To be a premier institution imparting quality education in engineering and technology to produce globally competent professionals.

Mission

  • To provide excellent academic and research environment.
  • To foster innovation and entrepreneurship.
  • To cultivate moral and ethical values among students.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  1. Apply knowledge of engineering fundamentals in real-world problem solving.
  2. Design and develop efficient software/hardware systems.
  3. Demonstrate leadership and teamwork in a diverse professional environment.
